Cosmotonic gives you the unique chance to see what astronauts do; whether that is educating you on space, showing you how they train, or launching you into space.


Our business is for outer space enthusiasts at any age. Children, adults, and elderly are able to learn about comets, planets, and stars in our family-friendly education center. They can also experience astronauts in training first hand to see what practice tests they do and so what. For a price, you can actually get launched into space! This is the only thing we offer that has an age range, which is 21-60 yo, for obvious reasons.
